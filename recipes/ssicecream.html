Sea Salt Ice Cream

Description

This ice cream mixes the salty taste of sea salt with the sweet tastes of cream and sugar.

Ingredients

  • A beach
  • Thickened cream
  • A history of mysterious weeb like behaviour

Steps

  1. Find a beach
  2. Pack the thickened cream in an esky
  3. Recall the time your PS2 Kingdom Hearts 2 disc stopped being readable because of a mysterious scratch
  4. Use the tears from that flashback to mix with your thickened cream
  5. Bury the cream under the sand
  6. Return 4 days later to discover your very own sea salt ice cream
